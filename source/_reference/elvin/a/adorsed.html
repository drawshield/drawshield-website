Adorsed

Adorsed, Addorsed, Adorssed, Adorse, Adosse, or Adossed. All these terms are better expressed by the word Endorsed.

These terms are all used by different writersto express the same thing, 1.e when any two bearings are placed back to back. P. 26, f. 15; p. 32, f. 29 ; p. 42, f. 10 and 12; p. 29, f. 44.
