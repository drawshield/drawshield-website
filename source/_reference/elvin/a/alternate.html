Alternate

Alternate, Alternately, or Alternatively. One after the other. Alternating. Following by turns. As an Orle of fleur-de-lis and martlets alternating, i.e. four fleur-de-lis and four martlets alternately placed. Amaranthal Crown. A crown like a garland composed of leaves of the imaginary amaranth that never fades. See Garland.
