Antelope

Antelope. An animal of the Deer kind, with two straight horns. P. 28, f. 22.

The Heraldic Antelope is a fabulous animal, and is represented as having the body of aStag ; the tail of a Unicorn ; a tusk issuing from the tip of the nose ; a row of tufts down the back of the neck, on the chest and thighs. Fig. 23.
