Basilisk

Basilisk. As represented in Heraldry resembles the heraldic wivern, but with the liead of a dragon at the end of the tail, and with the comb, wattles, and spurs of a Cock ; it is also termed the Amphisien-Cockatrice. P. 27, f. 10.
