Caduceus

Caduceus, or Mercury's Mace, or Wand ; termed sometimes Snakystaff, and Mercury's Soporiferous rod. The emblem of peace, depicted as P. 30, f. 56.

It is frequently born with a cap (called thePetasus P. 38, f. 4.) on the top of the staff.
