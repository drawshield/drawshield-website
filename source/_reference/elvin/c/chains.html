Chains

Chains. are frequently borne in the shield as a charge, or are attached to the Crest or Supporters as P. 21, f. 21 ; P. 18, f. 21.

A Cross of four chains square linked, fixedto an annulet in fesse P. 8, f. 11. A Saltire of Chains P. 20, f. 44. A Chain enarched, or in arch P. 42, f. 31. A Circular Chain and a circular chain within another. P. 42, f. 31.
