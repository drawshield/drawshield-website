Checky

Checky, Checkie,Chequy, Checkered, Chequered, Cheque, Chequee or Checquy. a term to express the field, or any bearing, when divided into small squares of alternate tinctures, and must consist of three or more rows, P. 2, f. 37. P. 4, f. 44. P. 7, f. 15. P. 19, f. 35.
