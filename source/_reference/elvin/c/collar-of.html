Collar of

Collar of, S.S. P. 24, f. 29.

The collar of S, or " of Esses," as it is writtenin many records, was a Lancastrian livery, and of the institution of Henry of Bolingbroke. This Collar is still worn by the Heralds, by the Lord Mayor of London, and by the Lord Chief Justices, and some others of the Judges, f . 29.
