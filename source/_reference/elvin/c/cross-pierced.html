Cross Pierced

Cross Pierced. The piercing is always in the middle, and if not otherwise expressed in circular. P. 8, f. 31. When the piercing is in any other form it must be named as lozengy. P. 10, f. 2. When square, or quarterly as P. 7, f. 16.
