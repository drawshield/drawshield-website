Crown of the Sovereign of Great Britain

Crown of the Sovereign of Great Britain. Is a circle of gold richly chased ornamented with pearls and stones, and heightened up with four crosses pattee, and four fleur-de-lis alternately ; from these rise four archdiadems, adorned with pearls, which close under a mound ensigned by a cross pattee, within the coronet a crimson cap, turned up Ermine. P. 24, f. 1._
