Duke's Mantle

Duke's Mantle. Is distinguished by having four guards or rows of fur on the dexter side. The engraved example of a mantle at P. 35, f. 16, is that of a Marquess, shewing three rows and half of fur. See Robe. Dung-fork. P. 39, f. 14, No. 1. Dung-hill Cock. See Cock. P. 34, f. 1.
