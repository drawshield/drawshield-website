Ensign

Ensign. A Flag. See also Banner, Flag, and Standard.

The ensign of the Royal Navy is white withSt. George's cross cantoned with the Union Jack. The Naval Reserve is blue with the Union Jack in the dexter chief. That of the Merdiant Service is a red flag with the Union Jack as the last. P. 25a, f. 5 and 6.
