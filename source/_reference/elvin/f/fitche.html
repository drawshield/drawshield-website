Fitche

Fitche, Fitchee, Fiche, Pitched, or Fitchy. From the Latin figo to fix or fasten ; a term applied to a cross, the lower extremity of which is sharpened to a point, to enable those Primitive Christians who originally carried them on their pilgrimages to easily fix them in the ground. P. 8, f. 19.
