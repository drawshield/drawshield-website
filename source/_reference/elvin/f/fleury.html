Fleury

Fleury, Fleurty, Floretty, Flurt, or Flury. Said of anything ending with a fleur-de-lis, sometimes termed Fleurette. P. 9, f. 13.

Differenced from the cross-fiory, by havinga line between the ends of the cross and the flowers. P. 10, f. 18.
