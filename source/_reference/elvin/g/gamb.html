Gamb

Gamb, Gambe, or Jambe. The whole fore leg of a beast. If couped, or erased near the middle joint, it is called a paw. See Seal's Paw. P. 29, f. 52.

Bear's gamb erased. P. 29, f. 41.

Lion's gamb erect and erased, f. 45.Lion's gamb erased, holding a laurel branch fructed. f. 46.

Two Lions' gambs, supporting a crescent,f. 47. Two Lions' gambs in saltire. f. 48. Lion's gamb erased and erect, supporting a shield. P. 31, f. 21.
