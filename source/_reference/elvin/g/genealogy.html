da., or dau. daughter,

da., or dau. & coh. daughter and heiress,

fil. et. haer. son and heir,

h. heir.

hr. ap., or appart. heir apparent,

m., md., or mar. married,

na., or nat. born,

ob., or obt. died,

s. son.

s. & h. son and heir,

sp. or sepult. buried,

temp, in the time of.

unmar. unmarried,

viv. or vix. was living, or lived in the time of.
