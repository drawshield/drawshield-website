Goat

Goat. A quadruped. The Heraldic Goat is always of the kind as P. 28, f. 59. Goat's head erased, f. 60. If the bearing is an Assyrian, Indian, or Angola, Goat, it must be blazoned as such. P. 29, f. 1 and 2.
