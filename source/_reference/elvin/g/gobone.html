Gobone

Gobone, Gobony, or Gobonated. Is composed of two tinctures in equal divisions, as a Bend Gobony. P. 17, f. 40. If it consists of two rows of chequers, it is termed Countergobony or Counter-Componee, for Componee is the same as Gobony. P. 17, f. 41.
