Griffin

Griffin, Griffon, or Giyphon. A Fabulous animal. P. 27, f. 1.

It has the wings, fore feet and head of anEagle witli the addition of ears ; the body, hind legs, and tail of a Lion. When the Griffin is in the position of Eampant it is not to be so blazoned, but is said to be segreant. P. 27, f. 2. Griffin passant, f. 1. Griffin sejant, f. 3. Demi Griffin segreant. f. 4. Griffin's head erased, f. 5.
