Harp

Harp. A stringed instrument with triangular frame, the cords of which are distended in parallel directions from the upper part to one of its sides. It is the well-known ensign of the kingdom of Ireland. P. 3, f. 21. For other examples see P. 43, f. 19, and P. 31, f. 6.
