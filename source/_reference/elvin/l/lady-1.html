Lady

Lady. As a Crest and Supporter, is frequently met with in Coat-Armour, and is blazoned either as a Lady, Female figure, or Woman. P. 35, f. 6.

Three Ladies from the waist as in the Armsof the See of Oxford. P. 22, f. 15.
