Lambrequin

Lambrequin, or Lamequin. The mantle or hood, intervening between the helmet and Crest, alwa3's represented flotant. P. 2, f. 24 ; P. 8, f. 21 ; P. 13, f, 21 ; P. 16, f. 21. If charged with the Arms it is termed a Lambrequin Arnioyees.
