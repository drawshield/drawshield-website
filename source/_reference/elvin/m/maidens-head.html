Maiden's-Head

Maiden's-Head. Ahvays depicted as the head and neck of a woman couped below the breast,. A Maiden's-Head wreathed about the temples with a garland of roses, and crowned with an antique crown, as borne in the arms of the Mercers' Company, London. P. 22, f. 24.
