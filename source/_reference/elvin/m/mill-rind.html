Mill-rind

Mill-rind, Mill-rine, or Mill-ink. Is the iron affixed to the centre of the mill-stone, by which it is turned by the wheel ; also termed Fer-de-Moline. P. 38, f. 53 and 54.

The first shewn in the engraving is thatusually met with, the other examples are antique ones sometimes met with. See Mill-rind on the Mill-stone, f. 51.
