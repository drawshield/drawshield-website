Nebule

Nebule, Nebula, Nebuly, or Nebulee. Also termed Nebular and Nebrjose. A crooked line to which all the ordinaries and partition lines are subject ; it is intended to represent clouds, and is drawn as P. 3, f. 4 ; P. 12, f. 11 ; P. 19, f. 7.
