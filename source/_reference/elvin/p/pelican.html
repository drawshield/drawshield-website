Pelican

Pelican. P. 33, f. 37.

The Heraldic Pelican is represented witliher wings endorsed, if not mentioned to the contrary, her neck embowed, pecking her breast, from whence issue drops of blood; and in blazon is termed vulned, or vulning. When depicted in her nest is termed in her piety, f. 38. The Emblem of Charity.
