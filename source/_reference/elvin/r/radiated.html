Radiated

Radiated, Rayonated, or Rayonee. The same as Rayonee, Radiant, Rayonnant, and Rayoonne. Terms all used to express the same thing, viz., Rays, or shining beams issuing from an ordinary or charge. P. 7, f. 17 ; P. 12, f. 12.
