Removed

Removed. Shifted from its place as a chief removed, or lowered. P. 12, f. 31. A chev. removed. P. 15, f. 37.

If a Fesse, Chevron, Bend, etc, is placedhigher in the shield than its proper place it is termed Enhanced. P. 3, f. 6.
