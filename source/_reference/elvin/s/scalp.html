Scalp

Scalp. Skin of a man's head with the hair. P. 36, f. 18. Also the skin of the forehead of an animal. If the animal have horns they are attached to the scalp, as at P. 15, f. 21. A Stag's 3 scalp. A Bull's scalp. P. 31, f. 17. Hare's scalp. P. 31, f. 15.
