Semee

Semee, Senime, or Seme. Aspersed, or Powdered. P. 2, f. 38.

The terms Averlye, Gerattie, and Strewed,are also used for the same thing, which implies that the field. Charge, Crest, or Supporter, is strewed over with figures, such as roses, stars, etc. When strewed with fieur-de-lis it is then termed Semee-de-lis.
