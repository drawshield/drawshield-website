Shuttle

Shuttle. An instrument used by weavers, and borne in the arms of their Company P. 40, f. 2.

It is blazoned a Shuttle tipped and quilled,and is very genei-ally given as a bearing to those who have risen to affluence by it. As the family of Peel.
