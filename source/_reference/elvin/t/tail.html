Tail

Tail. The tail of the lion, and the tail of a Beaver, are sometimes borne in Coat Armour. P. 29, f. 50.

The following are the different names forthe Tails of several animals, viz. : That of the Deer is called single ; of the Boar, the wreath ; of the Fox, the brush ; of the Wolf, the stern ; and of the Hare and Conay the scut.
