Talbot

Talbot. A hound witn long ears, and of vary common use in Coat Armour. A Talbot Statant. P. 29, f. 13. A Talbot Sejant, 1.d. f. 15. A Demi Talbot Ramp. 1.d. f. 14. A Talbot's Head erased, 1.d. f. 16. A She-Talbot, borne by the name of Gottington.
