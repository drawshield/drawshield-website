Trinity

Trinity. This Heraldic device which represents the Holy Trinity in an azure field was the heraldic ensign of the monastery of Grey Friars, called Christ church, in the city of London. It is also blazoned " The Triangular Emblem of the Trinity with the legend." The field is generally gu. P. 22, f. 1.
