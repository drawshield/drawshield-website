Endorse

Endorse, endorce, or indorse: a diminutive of the pale, of which it is one-fourth, or according to some authorities, one-eight. It bears exactly the same relation to that ordinary as the cottice does to the bend. See Cross, §8.

Argent, a pale engrailed between two indorses sable--BELLASIS, Scotland.
