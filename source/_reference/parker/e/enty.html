Enty

Enty: a word adopted but by few writers from the French enté, a graft, and applied to the base of the shield when parted off by a line chevronwise: written by some heralds ampty. See Point: also Gusset.

Argent, on a chief enty[in more recent blazon 'indented'] azure five crosses croslet or--Raphe de WILSHERE.
