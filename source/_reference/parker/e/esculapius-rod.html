Esculapius' Rod

Esculapius' Rod is borne in coats of arms.

Per fesse or and sable, a lion rampant counterchanged, armed and langued gules, on a canton of the last an Esculapius' rod palewise entwined with serpents argent--M'WHIRTER.

Sable, on a chevron between three spear heads argent, two staves of Esculapius, chevron wise, each entwined by a serpent proper--JONES, co. Carnarvon.
