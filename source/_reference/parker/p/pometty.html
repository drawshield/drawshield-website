Pometty

Pometty: applied to the Cross, q.v. §29, terminated in pomel shaped knobs. See also arms of TROSSELL, under Fret.

Pometty: also of a Cross, q.v. §29, or escarboucle(q.v.) having a rounded excrescence on each arm.
