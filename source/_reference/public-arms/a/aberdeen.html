ABERDEEN, The Council of the County of. Has for ensigns armorial the following, viz., Quarterly first azure, three garbs or, for Buchan ; .'^econd azure, a bend between six cross crosslets fitchee or, for Mar ; third or, a fesse chequy azure and argent between three open crowns gules, for Garioch ; fourth azure three boars' heads couped or, for Gordon.

[Matriculated in Lyon Office the nth day of July 1890.]
