ACADEMY OF THE MUSES, in Covent Garden, London, called "Muses Mannerey." Argent, two bars wavy azure, on a chief of the second, a musicbook open or, between two swords in saltire of the first hilted and pommelled of the third. Crest — On a wreath of the colours, a Sagittarius in full speed proper, shooting with a bow or, and arrow argent. Supporters — (Dexter) a merman with two tails both proper, (sinister) a satyr proper. Motto — " Nihil inviata Minerva."

[Granted by Borough, Garter.]
