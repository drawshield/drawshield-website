ADVOCATES, Dean and Faculty of (Scotland). Gules, a balance or, and a sword argent hilted and pommelled of the second placed saltirewise, surmounted of an escutcheon also of the second, charged with a lion rampant, within a double treasure flory, counterflory of the first. In an escroll above the shield is inscribed this motto, " Suum cuique," and surrounding the whole achievement is a belt azure, buckled and edged or, having thereon these words, " Sigillum facultatis juridicse."

[Matriculated in Lyon Register, 6th Feb. 1856.]
