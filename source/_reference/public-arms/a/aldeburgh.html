ALDEBURGH (Suffolk). Has no armorial bearings ; but William Hervey, Clarenceu.x King of Arms, granted October 20, 1561, to the corporation for a seal the following, namely, A ship of three masts in full sail on the waves of the sea, the mainsail charged with a lion rampant.
