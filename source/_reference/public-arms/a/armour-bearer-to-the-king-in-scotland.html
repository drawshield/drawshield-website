ARMOUR-BEARER TO THE KING IN SCOTLAND, The Heritable.

Behind the shield two spears in saltire bearing on their points a Royal Helmet and a shield charged with the Royal Arms of Scotland all proper, " as the badge of the office of Heritable Armour-Bearer to the King."

[The arms of Smith, alias Seton, of Touch were so matriculated in Lyon Office, 1 77 1. But the office has passed to the family of Steuart of Allanton, and at their matriculation of arms, in 1815, a spear and helmet were added as charges upon their shield.
