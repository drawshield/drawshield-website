AUSTRIAN LEO SOCIETY (A Catholic Literary Society). Sable, a lion rampant or, armed gules, charged on the shoulder with an escutcheon of the arms of the Austrian Imperial Family (gules, a fess argent), and holding in its forepaws the triple papal cross argent (1892).
