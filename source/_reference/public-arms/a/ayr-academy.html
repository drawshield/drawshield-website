AYR ACADEMY (Ayr). Gules, rising from a sea undy argent and azure, a castle triple towered of the second, between the head of St John the Baptist on a charger on the dexter and an open book bearing this inscription, " Dominus illuminatio mea," on the sinister, all proper, on a chief of the second a holy lamb with cross, staff, and banner of Scotland all proper.

[Matriculated in Lyon Register, i8th February 1912, the fees being raised by subscription amongst former scholars.]
