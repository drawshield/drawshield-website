BEDFORD COLLEGE FOR WOMEN (London). Argent, between two flaunches paly bendy or and sable, a cross patee throughout gules, voided of the field, surmounted by an open book of the second, on a chief of the third an antique lamp gold inflamed proper.

[Granted, College of Arms, 13th August 1913.]
