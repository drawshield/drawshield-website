BERKSHIRE, County of. Has no armorial bearings. Upon a coloured sheet of the " Arms of the Counties of England and Wales," which has been published, it is credited with "Gules five heads affrontee in saltire argent couped in some peculiar manner below the shoulders vested azure and crowned (with most peculiar coronets) or." This is evidently a perversion of the seal and arms of Reading.
