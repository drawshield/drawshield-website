BOWYERS, Worshipful Company of (London). Sable, on a chevron or, between three floats argent, as many mullets pierced of the first. Crest — On a wreath or and azure, three long-bows interlaced one erect and two in saltire gules, stringed or. Motto — " Crecy, Poitiers, Agincourt." Mantled, sable, furred ermine. [Arms and crest granted by Holme, Ciarenceux, 4 Henry VH., 1489.]
