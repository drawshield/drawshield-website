BRADFIELD COLLEGE. Uses a device, viz., Within a circle inscribed with the words, " Coll. S. Andrese. Bradfield. Berks," a saltire gules entwined by a scroll, thereon the motto, " Benedictus es, O Domine Doce me statuta tua." [Of no authority.]
