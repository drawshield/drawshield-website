BRIDPORT (Dorsetshire). Gules, a castle with two towers argent, over each a fleur-de-lis or, in chief a lion passant guardant, crowned of the last, the base barry wavy of eight of the second and azure : in the portway three. . . . Recorded in the College of Arms.
