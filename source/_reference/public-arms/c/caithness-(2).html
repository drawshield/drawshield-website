CAITHNESS, County of. Has no armorial bearings. The seal of the County Council exhibits the crest (a cock proper) and the Motto ("Commit thy work to God ") of the Earl of Caithness. The arms of the old Earldom of Caithness, which form a part of Lord Caithness's achievement, are " Azure a ship under sail or."
