CALCUTTA, See of. Per fesse indented ermine and gules, a crosier in bend or, headed argent surmounted of an open book proper in base, two palm branches in saltire vert, surmounted of a mitre gold in chief. [Granted 1814, College of Arms, Gts. 28, 204.]
