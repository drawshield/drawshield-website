CANARY COMPANY. (Incorporated 17th March 1664.) Argent, a cross gules, on a chief azure a lion passant guardant, between two bunches of grapes stepped or. Crest — On a wreath of the colours, a mountain as representing the Peak of Teneriffe proper. Supporters — Two falcons with wings endorsed or, belled of the last.

[Granted by Walker, Garter,. 1665.]
