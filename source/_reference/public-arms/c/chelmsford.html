CHELMSFORD (Essex). Argent, a bridge of three arches proper, in chief two croziers in saltire between as many lions rampant azure, in base two bars wavy of the last. Crest — Upon a rock proper, a crozier in pale or, surmounted by two swords in saltire points upwards proper, pommels and hilts or, interlaced by a wreath of oak vert. Motto — " Many minds one heart." [Granted, College of Arms, February 6, 1889.]
