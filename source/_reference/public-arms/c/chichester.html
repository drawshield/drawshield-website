CHICHESTER (Sussex). Argent, gutt^e-de-poix, on a chief indented gules, a lion passant guardant or. Recorded in the College of Arms.

A manuscript in Ulster's Office shows the arms as per fesse argent and chequy or and gules, in chief a tower triple-towered azure. It would be interesting to know the origin of this.
