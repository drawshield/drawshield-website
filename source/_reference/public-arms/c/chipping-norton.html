CHIPPING NORTON (Oxfordshire). Has no armorial bearings. The seal represents upon a mount a castle, the two towers each surmounted by a cupola and flag, and above the centre battlements the letters I.R. The legend is

" Sigil. Burg, de Chippingnorton. Feby. 1606."
