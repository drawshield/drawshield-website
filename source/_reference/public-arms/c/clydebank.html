CLYDEBANK. Has no arms. The seal shows a fine healthy specimen of homemade heraldry, viz., Argent, a saltire gules, in chief a sewing-machine, in base a battle-ship, in fesse on the dexter a stag's head caboshed, and on the sinister a lion rampant. Crest — A garb. Motto — " Lahore et scientia."
