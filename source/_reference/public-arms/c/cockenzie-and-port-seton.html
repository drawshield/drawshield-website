COCKENZIE AND PORT SETON. Has no arms. The seal shows three escutcheons : {a) the Royal Arms of Scotland, {b) the arms of Seton, viz., three crescents within the double tressure, {c) a representation of Preston Tower. Between the escutcheons are a swan (the crest of the Earl of Wemyss), a stag's head couped (the crest of Cadell), and an anchor.
