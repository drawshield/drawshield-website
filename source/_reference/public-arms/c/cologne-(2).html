COLOGNE, Elector and Archbishop of. Quarterly: i, argent, a cross sable (for the archbishopric of Cologne), 2, gules, a horse salient argent (Westphalia), 3, gules, three human hearts, two and one, or (for Engern), 4, azure, an eagle displayed argent (for Arensberg).
