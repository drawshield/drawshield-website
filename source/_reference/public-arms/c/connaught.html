CONNAUGHT, Province of (Ireland). Per pale argent and azure, on the dexter a dimidiated eagle displayed sable, and on the sinister conjoined therewith at the shoulder a sinister arm embowed proper ; sleeved of the first, holding a sword erect also proper.

[Recorded in Ulster's Office.]
