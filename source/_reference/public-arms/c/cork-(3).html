CORK, See of Argent, a cross patt^e gules, charged with a crozier in pale, enfiled with a mitre labelled or.

[This coat, which is recorded in Ulster's Office, remains in use, but through the disestablishment of the Irish Church it is really extinct, and its present use is illegal.]
