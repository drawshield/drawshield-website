CORPUS CHRISTI COLLEGE (Cambridge). (Founded in 1351, by the Aldermen and Guild of Cambridge.) Qrly. i and 4, gules, a pelican in her piety argent, vulning her breast proper, 2 and 3, azure, three lilies argent, two and one.

[Granted 23rd December 1570, College of Arms.]
