CULROSS (Perthshire). Has not matriculated any armorial bearings. The seal represents a church (Cat. of Her. Exn. says that of St Serf), in the doorway of which is standing a figure with hands clasped in prayer : above the doorway upon an escrol being the inscription " S. Servanus." The legend is " Sigillum commune burgi de Culros."
