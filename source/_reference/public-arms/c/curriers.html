CURRIERS, The Worshipful Company of (London). (Incorporated 30th April 1606). Azure, a cross engrailed or, between four pairs of currier's shaves in saltire argent, handled of the second. Mantle — Gules, double argent. Crest — On the wreath of the colours, two arms embowed proper, vested to the elbows argent issuing from clouds of the first, holding in the hands a shave as in the arms. Supporters — (Dexter) an elk proper, attired and unguled or, (sinister) a goat argent, armed and unguled or. Motto — " Spes nostra Deus." [Recorded in the College of Arms. Misc. Gts., i. 115 b.\
