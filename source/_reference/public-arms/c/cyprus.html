CYPRUS. Although Cyprus is administered by the United Kingdom it is really part of the Ottoman Empire, and no power exists in this country to assign arms to it. But the Admiralty publish for use by the High Commissioner of Cyprus upon the Union Flag a white disc showing two lions passant guardant in pale gules.
