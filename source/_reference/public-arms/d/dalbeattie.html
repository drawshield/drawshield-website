DALBEATTIE (Kirkcudbrightshire). Has no arms. Those in use are those of the old Earls of Nithsdale, viz., Argent, an eagle with two heads displayed sable, beaked and membercd gules ; on the breast an escutcheon charged with a saltire sa., surcharged with an urcheon, between in chief a tree, and in the flanks and base a mullet. Crest — A stag lodged under a holly bush. Motto — " Respice prospice."

[Of no authority.]
