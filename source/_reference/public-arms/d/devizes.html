DEVIZES (Wiltshire). Party per pale gules and azure, a castle in perspective, the whole forming a hexagon, the front triple-towered, and the two outer towers domed all or, each dome surmounted by an estoile sable.

Recorded in the Visitation Books at the College of Arms.
