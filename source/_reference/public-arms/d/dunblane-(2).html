DUNBLANE (Perthshire). Has no armorial bearings. The design upon the burgh seal is suggested by an old ecclesiastical seal. On the dexter side is St Laurence, and on the sinister side is a bishop (? St Blane), mitred and robed, his dexter hand raised in the action of benediction, and his sinister holding his crosier.
