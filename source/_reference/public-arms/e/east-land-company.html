EAST LAND COMPANY. (Incorporated tcvip. Elizabeth and Charles I., confirmed by Charles II.). Or, on the sea in base a ship of three masts in full sail all proper, the sails, pennants, and ensigns argent charged with a cross gules, on a chief of the last a lion passant guardant of the first. Crest — On a wreath of the colours, an allocamelus, or ass-camel proper. Supporters — Two bears proper. Motto— •' Despair not." [Of no authority.]
