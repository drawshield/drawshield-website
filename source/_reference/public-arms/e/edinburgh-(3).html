EDINBURGH, See of. Azure, a saltire argent, in chief a mitre of the second, garnished or.

[These arms were matriculated in Lyon Register in 1674 and are still in use, but by the disestablishment of the Episcopal Church in Scotland, they are really extinct and their present use is improper.]
