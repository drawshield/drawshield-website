ELPHIN, See of. Sable, two crosiers indorsed in saltire or, in base a lamb couchant argent, in chief a mitre of the second.

[This coat is recorded in Ulster's Office, but through the disestablishment of the Irish Church it is really extinct and its present use is illegal.]
