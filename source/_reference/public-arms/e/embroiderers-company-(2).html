EMBROIDERERS' COMPANY (Bristol), Gules, two broaches in saltire argent, between two bundles or, on a chief of the second, a lion passant gules.

[Previously the arms in use were those of the Broderers' Company of London, to which refer. There is no authority for the use of either by the Bristol Company.]
