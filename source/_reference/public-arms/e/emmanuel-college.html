EMMANUEL COLLEGE (Cambridge). (Founded in 1584 by Sir Walter Mildmay, Knt., Chancellor and Treasurer of the Exchequer.) Argent, a lion rampant azure, holding in the dexter paw a chaplet of laurel vert, in chief a scroll sable thereon the word Emmanuel gold.

[Recorded in College of Arms. Granted ist January 1588-9.]
