EPSOM COLLEGE (Epsom, Surrey). Per pale azure and sable, three fleurs-delis or, on a chief of the last, an open book proper, inscribed with the words,

" Olim meminisse juvabit," between in the dexter a lamp and in the sinister a Rod of jEsculapius, gules. Crest — On a wreath of the colours, in front of an eagle's head, between two wings azure, three fleurs-de-lis gold. Motto — •" Deo non Fortuna."

[Granted, College of Arms, June 7, 1910.]
