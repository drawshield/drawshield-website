ERITH URBAN DISTRICT COUNCIL (Kent). Argent, a fleur-de-lis sable between three lucies haurient two and one gules, on a canton of the last a horse forcene of the field Crest — On a wreath of the colours, in front of a garb or, a stag courant gules. Motto — " Labour overcomes all things." [Granted, College of Arms, February 27, 1906.]
