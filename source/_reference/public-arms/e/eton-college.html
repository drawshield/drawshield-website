ETON COLLEGE. Sable, three lilies slipped argent two and one, a chief per pale azure and gules, on the dexter side a fleur-de-lis and on the sinister a lion passant guardant or.

[Granted by Letters Patent under the Great Seal by King Henry VI., 1st January 1449. Grant printed " Excerpta Historica," 47.]
