F. R. the dexter claw holding an orb azure, banded and surmounted by a cross also or, the sinister claw holding a sceptre (for Prussia) : sinister, argent, an eagle displayed gules with Sachsen or, on the head an Electoral Bonnet proper on the breast an escutcheon azure, charged with a sceptre in pale or, the dexter claw holding a sceptre and the sinister a sword proper (for Brandenburg) on an inescutcheon in base, surmounted by a mural crown or, the old arms of the city of Berlin, namely argent, a bear rampant sable.
