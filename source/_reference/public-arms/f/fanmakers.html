FANMAKERS (or Fan-stickmakers), The Worshipful Company of (London). (Incorporated 19th April 1709.) Or, a fan displayed with a mount of various device and colours, the sticks gules : on a chief per pale gules and azure, on the dexter side a shaving iron over a bundle of fan-sticks tied together or, on the sinister side a framed saw in pale of the last. Crest — On a wreath of the colours, a hand couped proper, holding a fan displayed or. Motto — "Arts and Trades united."
