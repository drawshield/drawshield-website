FARRIERS, The Worshipful Company of (London). (Incorporated 17th January 1684.) Argent, three horse-shoes sable, pierced of the field. Crest — On a wreath of the colours, an arm embowed issuing from clouds on the sinister side all proper, holding in the hand a hammer azure, handled and ducally crowned or. Supporters — Two horses argent. Motto — " Vi et virtute." [Of no authority.]
