FETHARD (Co. Tipperary). Has no armorial bearings. The seal, an impression of which has come under the editor's notice, is quadrilateral in shape, simply showing upon a mount a stag trippant within the legend "The Corporation of Fethard Seal." But Burke in his " General Armory " says, " The Seal is a stag standing before a tree ppr."
