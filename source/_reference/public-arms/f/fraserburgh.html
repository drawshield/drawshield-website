FRASERBURGH (Aberdeenshire). Has no arms. Those on the seal are taken from the arms of Lord Saltoun, viz. : i and 4, azure, three cinquefoils ; 2, or, a lion rampant gules debruised by a riband sable ; 3, gules, a lion rampant. Crest — An ostrich holding in its beak a key. Supporters — Two angels. Motto —

" In God is all."
