FRENCH MERCHANTS' COMPANY. (Incorporated by Edward IV.) Quarterly azure and gules, in the first and fourth quarters a fleur-de-lis or, in the second and third quarters a lion passant guardant of the last, over all a cross argent. Crest — On a wreath of the colours, a lion rampant guardant or, supporting an anchor sable, beamed of the first. Supporters — Two dolphins proper, ducally crowned and finned or. [Recorded in College of Arms.]
