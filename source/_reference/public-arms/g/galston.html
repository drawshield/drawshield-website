GALSTON (Ayrshire). Has no armorial bearings. Those upon the seal are azure, a cross moline argent, on a chief of the last a pick and shovel in saltire proper. Crest — Two shuttles in saltire proper. Motto—" Lahore et fiducia." [Of no authority.]
