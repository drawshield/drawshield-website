GARTER PRINCIPAL KING OF ARMS. Argent, a cross gules, on a chief azure, a ducal coronet encirled with a garter between a lion passant guardant on the dexter and a fleur-de-lis on the sinister, all or.

[These arms of office are either borne alone or impaled on the de.xter side of the personal arms of Garter.

The escutcheon is surmounted by his official crown, and behind it in bend is placed a representation of his sceptre of silver gilt.]
