GIRDLERS, The Worshipful Company of, London, (Incorporated loth March 1327.) Per fesse azure and or, a pale counterchanged and three gridirons of the last, the handles in chief. Alantliiig — Azure, lined ermine. Crest — On a wreath of the colours, a demi-man proper, representing St Lawrence, with a glory round his head or, issuing out of clouds of the first, vested azure, girt round the body with a girdle of the second, holding in his dexter hand a gridiron of the last and in the sinister a book argent. Motto — " Give thanks to God."

[Granted by John Smert, Garter, 1454.]
