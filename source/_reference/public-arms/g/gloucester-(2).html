GLOUCESTER, Dean of. Azure on a fesse or, three crosses pattee fitch^e of the first, on a quarter (or canton) of the second issuant to the dexter and sinister a demi fleur-de-lis conjoined to the side of the first, and issuant in chief a demisun in splendour argent. [The authority for the foregoing is doubtful.] Woodward gives, Argent, three chevrons gules between ten torteaux. [Of no authority at all.]
