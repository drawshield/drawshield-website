GOULBURN, See of (Australia). Gules, a Paschal Lamb passant upon a mount, above it an open book with seven seals proper : on a chief or, between two doves each holding a sprig of olive in its beak proper, a pale azure charged with four estoiles in cross argent. [Of no authority.]
