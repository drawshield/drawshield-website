GRANTON, Port and Harbour of. (The Duke of Buccleuch as proprietor of) Parted per pale, the de.xter side parted per fesse argent and or, in chief a merchant ship with three masts at anchor in a harbour proper, in base an anchor gules : the sinister side quarterly i and 4 or, on a bend azure, a mullet between two crescents of the field, 2 gyronny of eight or and sable, 3 argent, a galley, oars in action sable, flagged gules.

[Matriculated in Lyon Register, 1866.]
