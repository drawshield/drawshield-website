HANNOVER, Town of (Hanover, Germany). Gules, upon a battlemented wall surmounted by two towers argent, a lion passant or, armed and langued azure : in the open portway of the wall below the raised portcullis an inescutcheon or, charged with a clover-leaf vert, the point of the leaf towards the base seeded and veined also or. Mantling — Gules and or. Crest — Upon a wreath gules and or, between two bufTalo horns the de.xter per fesse gules and or, the sinister counterchanged, a clover-leaf as in the arms. Supporters — Two lions or.
