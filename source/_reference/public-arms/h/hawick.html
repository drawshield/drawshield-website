HAWICK (Roxburghshire). Has not matriculated any armorial bearings. The seal represents an escutcheon charged with an altar surmounted by a book between, on the dexter side a banner bearing the date I5i4> and on the sinister side a heart regally crowned, on a chief sable a lamp. The legend is " Sigillum Burgi de Hawick."
