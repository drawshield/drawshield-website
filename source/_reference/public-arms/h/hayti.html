HAYTI. Azure, on a mount in front of a palm-tree surmounted by a cap of liberty, a trophy of military weapons. [Refer to illustration.]

Christopher, the black Emperor of Hayti, assumed the following arms : Or, a phoenix imperially crowned issuing from flames proper. Motto — " Je renais de mes cendres." Supporters — Two lions rampant guardant ermine, imperially crowned or.
