HESSE, Grand Duchy of. Azure, a lion rampant double-queued barry of eight argent and gules, crowned or, holding in his dexter paw a sword of the second, hilt and pommel gold. Supporters — Two lions guardant queue-fourche^ and crowned or. Motto — "Gott ehre vaterland."
