HOHENZOLLERN LAND, Province of (Prussia). Quarterly argent and sable. Crest — Out of a crown a talbot's head or, eared gules. Supporters — (Dexter) a savage supporting a banner of Prussia ; (sinister) a man in complete armour supporting a banner of Hohenzollern.
