HOLY SPIRIT, College of the (Isle of Cumbrae, N.B.). Quarterly, ist and 4th grand quarters, azure, St Columba in a boat at sea, on his sinister hand a dove, and in dexter chief a blazing star all proper ; 2nd and 3rd grand quarters, counterquartered, 1st and 4th or, an eagle displayed with two heads gules, armed and beaked azure, 2nd and 3rd, parted per bend embattled gules and argent ; in an escutcheon of pretence in the centre of the 2nd and 3rd grand quarters or, three stags' horns gules.

[Recorded in Lyon Office. Granted by George Burnett, Lyon King of Arms, 30th November 1874.]
