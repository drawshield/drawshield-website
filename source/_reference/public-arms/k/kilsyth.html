KILS.YTH. Has no arms, but has a fearful and wonderful seal divided into quarters : i an open book, 2 two claymores in saltire, points downwards, 3 two weavers' shuttles in saltire, 4 a pit head, over all an inescutcheon, per pale dexter three gilly-flowers, sinister three crescents within a double tressure. [Bogus.]
