KILWINNING (Ayrshire). Has not matriculated any armorial bearings. The seal represents under a Gothic canopy a figure of St Winning (a Scottish saint of the eighth century), holding in his dexter hand a crozier, and in his sinister a closed book. Legend, " Burgh of Kilwinning. Sine Te Domine cuncta nil."
