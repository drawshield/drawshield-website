LICHFIELD, See of. Per pale gules and argent, a cross potent quadrate in the centre per pale of the last and or, between four crosses pattee, those on the dexter argent, those on the sinister or.

[Recorded in the College of Anns.]

The foregoing are the correct arms of the see, but they are generally quoted and used as per pale gules and argent, a cross potent quadrate in the centre between four crosses pattee all coiinterchanged.
