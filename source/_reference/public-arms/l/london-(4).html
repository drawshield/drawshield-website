LONDON, The Guild of Freemen of the City of. Argent, on a cross gules enfiled in chief and base by two mural crowns or, a rose of the first, slipped and leaved proper. Crest — A mural crown or, rising therefrom a dove, wings expanded proper. Motto — " Londini defendi tuos deus optime cives." [Granted, College of Arms, 31st May 191 2.]
