LONDON COUNTY COUNCIL. Barry wavy of six azure and argent, on a chief of the last, the cross of St George, charged with a lion of England : the shield ensigned with a mural crown gold.

[Granted by H.M.'s Royal Warrant, 29th July 1914, and exemplified in the College of Arms.]
