LORDS-LIEUTENANT OF COUNTIES. His Majesty, April 27, 191 1, approved of a flag to be used by Lords-Lieutenant, viz., the Union flag charged on the cross of St George with a sword fesseways, point to the sinister, surmounted by an Imperial Crown proper.
