LORNE, Lordship of. Argent, a galley (or lymphad) sable, sails furled, flag and pennants flying and oars in action proper.

[This coat, matriculated in Lyon Register, is borne for the Lordship of Lome by the Dukes of Argyll quarterly (in the second and third quarters) with the arms of Campbell.]
