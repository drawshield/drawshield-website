LOUTH (Lincolnshire). Has no armorial bearings. As to a seal, the Town-Clerk returned the letter of the editor asking for an impression of the seal, with the curt remark, "We have none," superscribed upon it. A Corporation without a seal, one is inclined to think, must be unique.
