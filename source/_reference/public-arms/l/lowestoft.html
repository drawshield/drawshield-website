LOWESTOFT (Suffolk). Argent, on a chevron sable, between in chief an antique crown between two roses gules, each rose charged with another rose argent, all barbed and seeded proper, and in base a sun issuant or, three Lowestoft china plates also proper. Crest — On a wreath of the colours, a demi-figure representing St Margaret, holding in the hand a pearl all proper. Motto — " Point du jour."

[Granted, College of Arms, 14th February 1913.]
