LUTON (Bedfordshire). Quarterly gules and azure, on a cross argent, between a garb in the first quarter, a beehive in the second, a rose slipped and leaved in the third, and a thistle also slipped and leaved in the fourth, all proper, a bee volant of the last. And for the Crest — On a wreath of the colours upon a mount vert, a cubit arm in bend, vested azure, cuff argent, the hand proper, holding seven ears of wheat or. Motto — " Scientize et labori detur." Granted, College of Arms, 25th July 1876.
