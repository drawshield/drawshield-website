MAGDALEN COLLEGE (Oxford). (Founded in the year 1456 by William Patten, or, as he was otherwise called from the place of his nativity, William of Wainfleet, Bishop of Winchester.) Lozengy ermine and sable on a chief of the last three lilies argent, slipped and seeded or.

[Recorded College of Arms, Visitation of Oxford, 1574.]
