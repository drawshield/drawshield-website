MAGDALEN COLLEGE (Cambridge). (Founded in 1541 by Thomas Audley, Baron IValden, and Lord Chancellor of England.) Quarterly, per pale indented or and azure in the 2nd and 3rd quarters an eagle displayed of the first, on a bend of the second a fret between two martlets of the first. [Of no authority.]
