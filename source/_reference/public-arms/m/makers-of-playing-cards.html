MAKERS OF PLAYING CARDS, The Worshipful Company of, London. (Incorporated 28th October 1628.) Gules, on a cross argent, between the four ace cards proper the aces of hearts and diamonds in chief and of clubs and spades in base, a lion passant guardant of the first. Crest — On a wreath of the colours, an armed arm erect holding in the hand an ace of hearts all proper. Supporters — Two men in complete armour proper, garnished or, on each a sash gules.
