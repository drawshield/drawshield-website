MANCHESTER AND SALFORD BANK. Azure, a garb or, banded gules, a bordure argent, charged with five torteaux, on a chief of the second, three bendlets of the third. Crest — On a wreath of the colours a demi-eagle displayed with two heads vert, each wing charged with a bezant and on the breast a trefoil slipped or. Motto — " Respice et prospice." [Granted, College of Arms, 1875.]
