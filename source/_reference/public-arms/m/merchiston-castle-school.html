MERCHISTON CASTLE SCHOOL (Edinburgh). Has no armorial bearings. Those in use are. Argent, a saltire engrailed between four roses gules. Crest — A hand proper, holding a crescent or. Motto — " Ready, aye ready." [These, of course, are the arms of Napier of Merchiston.]
