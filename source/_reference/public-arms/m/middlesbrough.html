MIDDLESBROUGH, Borough of (Yorkshire). Argent, a Hon rampant azure, on a chief sable, three ships in full sail or, sails of the first. Cirst — On a mural crown or, charged with three anchors erect sable, a lion passant azure. Motto —

" Erimus."

[Granted, College of Arms, November 8, 191 1.]
