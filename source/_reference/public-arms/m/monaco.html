MONACO. Fusilly argent and gules. Supporters — Two monks vested in long robes sable, mantles argent, each holding a sword all proper. Crest — Out of a marquis's coronet or, a fleur-de-lis of the last, between two branches, viz., on the dexter, a palm, on the sinister, a laurel, both proper. Motto^-" Deo juvante." [These are the family arms of Grimaldi, Princes of Monaco.]
