NEWTOWN or FRANVILLE (Hants). Has no armorial bearings. The seal, which is very ancient, represents an antique ship on the sea with one mast, sail furled and pennon flying ; on the ship a lion passant guardant, in chief on the dexter a mullet, and on the sinister a crescent ; in fesse on the sinister side an escutcheon of St George.
