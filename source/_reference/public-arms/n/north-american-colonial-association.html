NORTH AMERICAN COLONIAL ASSOCIATION. Quarterly, ist, argent, a ship of three masts on the sea, in full sail proper ; 2nd, on a mount a beaver, and in the distance a forest, all proper; 3rd, gules, a plough or ; 4th, azure a garb or. On an escocheon in centre point argent a trefoil slipped vert royal crowned of England proper. Crest — On a wreath of the colours an Irish wolf dog couchant proper. Supporters — (Dexter) an Irish peasant habited, jacket azure, trousers argent, his hat of straw, holding over his dexter shoulder a felling axe proper ; (sinister) a similar figure of an Irishman holding in his left hand a reaping-hook or sickle proper. Motto — " Magnum vectigal industris."

[Granted, 6th October 1S35, by Sir William Betham, Ulster.]
