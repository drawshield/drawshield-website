NORTH-WEST TERRITORIES (Province, Dominion of Canada). No warrant assigning any arms has ever been issued either for the Province or for the districts of Assiniboia, Athabasca, Keewatin, Yukon, Mackenzie, Ungava, or Franklin, which now make up the Province.
