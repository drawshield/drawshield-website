NOTTINGHAMSHIRE. Has no armorial bearings. The seal of the County Council, however, exhibits these arms, " Quarterly argent and or, on a cross raguly gules between in the first quarter, a tree eradicated, in the second, a pickaxe and spade in saltire, handles downwards, and pendent therefrom a safetylamp, in the third quarter, a representation of a lace-making machine (?), and in the fourth quarter a garb, all proper, a ducal coronet of the second.
