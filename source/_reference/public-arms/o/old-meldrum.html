OLD MELDRUM. Has no arms but borrows the entire achievement of Urquhart of Meldrum, viz., " Or, three boars' heads gules." Crest — A demi-otter crowned with an antique crown and holding between its paws a crescent. Mottoes — (Over crest) " Per mare per terras " ; (under arms) " Mean, speak and doe well." Supporters — Two greyhounds proper, collared gules, leashed or.
