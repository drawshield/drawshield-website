ORIEL COLLEGE (Oxford). (Founded 1323, by Adam le Brome, Confessor to Edward II.) Gules three lions passant guardant in pale or, a bordure engrailed argent.

[Recorded in the College of Arms at the Visitation of the County of Oxford, 1574.]
