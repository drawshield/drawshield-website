PARISH CLERKS, The Worshipful Company of, London. (Incorporated 1232.) Azure, a fleur-de-lis or, on a chief gules, a leopard's face between two song books (closed) of the second, stringed vert. Crest — On a wreath of the colours, a cubit arm erect vested azure, cuffed ermine, holding in the hand proper a music book (open) of the last, garnished or, stringed vert. Motto — " Unitas Societatis Stabilitas."

[Granted 30th March 15S2. Confirmed, approved, and entered by Henry St George, at the Visitation of London, 1634]. Supporters are used, viz. on either side an angel holding by the interior hand and blowing a trumpet, but these are of no authority.]
