PAVIORS, The Worshipful Company of, London. Argent, a chevron between three flagstones sable. Crest — On a wreath of the colours, an arm embowed vested azure, cuffed argent, holding in the hand proper a pickaxe of the last. Motto — " God can raise to Abraham children of stones." [Of no authority.]
