PETERBOROUGH (Northamptonshire). Azure, two keys endorsed in saltire or. [Recorded in the College of Arms.]

Burke in his " General Armory " gives, Gules, two keys endorsed in saltire between four cross crosslets fitchee. Berry also gives this coat, and adds a note : " Peterborough uses for its Arms those of the Deanery, the Dean and Chapter being Lords of the Manor."
