PEWTERERS (Gateshead). Azure, on a chevron or, between three antique limbecks argent, as many roses gules. Crest — Two arms embowed proper, holding in both arms erect a dish argent. Supporters — Two sea-horses or, tails proper. Motto — " In God is all my trust."

[Of no authority. Taken from the Gateshead Charter, 1671.]
