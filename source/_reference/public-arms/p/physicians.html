PHYSICIANS, Royal College of (London). (Incorporated by Henry VIII., a.d. 1523.) Sable, a hand proper vested argent, issuing out of clouds in chief of the second rayonnee or, feeling the pulse of an arm proper issuing from the sinister side of the shield vested argent, in base a pomegranate or, between five demi fleurs-de-lis bordering the edge of the escutcheon of the last. [Recorded in College of Arms.]
