POMERANIA, Province of (Prussia). Argent, a griffin segreant gules, armed or. Mantling — Gules and argent. Crest — Out of a coronet or, a plume of peacock's feathers proper. Supporters — (Dexter) a wild man wreathed about the head and middle with oak-leaves, supporting with his dexter hand a banner of Prussia ; (sinister) a man in complete armour, supporting with his exterior hand a banner of Pomerania.
