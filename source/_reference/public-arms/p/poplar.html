POPLAR, Borough of (London). Has no arms. The seal shows a device of three escutcheons, thereon, (a) a gateway, {b) a bridge, between two bars on each a bow stringed, (c) a human figure vested, the right hand raised in benediction, the sinister holding a crosier.
