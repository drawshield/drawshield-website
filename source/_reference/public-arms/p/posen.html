POSEN (Poland). Gules, on a mount in base vert a castle proper, in the open gateway a key in bend sinister or, surmounted by a key in bend argent, from each of the outer towers a human figure habited, the head within a glory or and the sinister figure holding in his dexter hand a key also or, in chiel an eagle displayed argent, crowned or.
