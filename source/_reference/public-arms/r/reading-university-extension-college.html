READING, UNIVERSITY EXTENSION COLLEGE. Per fesse gules and sable, in chief three escallops fessewise or and in base on a cross engrailed argent, a rose of the first, barbed and seeded proper.

[Granted 7th August 1896.]

The engrailed cross was suggested by the arms of Christ Church, Oxford, and the escallops by the arms of Reading Abbey.
