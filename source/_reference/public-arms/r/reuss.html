REUSS, Principality of. Quarterly : i and 4, sable, a lion rampant or, crowned gules; 2 and 3, argent, a stork or. Crests — i. On a crown, a plume of ostrich feathers ; 2, a dog's head per pale argent and sable ; 3, a stork bendy or, argent and gules. Supporters — Two lions regardant per fesse sable and argent. Motto — " Ich bau auf Gott."
