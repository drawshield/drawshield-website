REVELS, Master of in Scotland. Argent, a lady rising out of a cloud in the nombril point richly apparelled, on her head a garland of ivy, holding in her right hand a poniziard crowned, and in the left a vizard all proper, standing under a veil or canopy azure, garnished or, in base a thistle vert. [Matriculated in Lyon Register.]
