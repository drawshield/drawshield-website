RHODES UNIVERSITY COLLEGE. Or, on a pile sable, an open book inscribed with the words " Sapientiam exquiret sapiens" between three escallops of the first, on a chief argent, a lion passant gules, between two thistles slipped and leaved proper. Crest — Upon a rock the figure of a man mounted on a horse, representing " Energy," all argent. Motto — " Vis virtus Veritas." [Granted, College of Arms, 5th May 191 3.]
