RIGA (Russia). Azure, on a compartment or, in front of a double-headed eagle displayed sable, crowned and armed or, a castellated gateway gules, on each tower a banner or and in the open gateway a lion's face crowned, also or, above the castle two keys addorsed in saltire wards upwards surmounted by a cross patt6e or, on the centre chief point the Russian imperial crown.
