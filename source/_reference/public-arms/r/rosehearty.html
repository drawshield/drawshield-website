ROSEHEARTY. Has no arms. Those upon the seal are : Quarterly, i and 4, azure, three boars' heads couped argent ; 2 and 3, gules, three cinquefoils argent. Crest— On a baron's cap and coronet, a rose-branch and a heart. Supporlcrs — Two bears proper, muzzled gules. Motto — " Cordo et manu."
