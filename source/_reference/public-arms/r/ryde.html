RYDE (Isle of Wight). Argent, in base on waves of the sea a schooner yacht under sail proper, within a bordure azure, charged with eight estoiles or. Crest — Upon a wreath of the colours, upon a rock a sea-horse proper, charged on the body with two estoiles or. Motto — " Amoenitas salubritas urbanitas."

[Granted by Sir Charles George Young, Knt., Garter Principal King of Arms ; Robert Laurie, Clarenceu.x King of Arms ; Walter Aston Blount, Norroy King of Arms, iSth February 1869.]
