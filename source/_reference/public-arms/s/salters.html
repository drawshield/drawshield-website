SALTERS, The Worshipful Company of (London). (Incorporated 1559.) Per chevron azure and gules, three covered salts argent, garnished or. Crest — On a wreath of the colours, a cubit arm erect, issuing from clouds all proper, holding a covered salt argent, garnished or. Supporters — Two otters sable, bezanty, ducally gorged and chained or. Motto — " Sal sapit omnia."

[Arms granted by Thomas Benolt, Clarenceux, 1530, and crest and supporters by Robert Cooke, Clarenceux, 1591 ; confirmed at the visitation of the City of London, 1634.]
