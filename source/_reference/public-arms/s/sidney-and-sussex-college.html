SIDNEY AND SUSSEX COLLEGE (Cambridge). (Founded in 1 595 by Frances, daughter of Sir William Sidney, Knt, and widow of Thomas Radcliff, Earl of Sussex.) Argent, a bend engrailed sable for Radcliff, impaling Or, a pheon azure, for Sidney.

[Granted by Sir Edward Walker, Garter, 1675.]
