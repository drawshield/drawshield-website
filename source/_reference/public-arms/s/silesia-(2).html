SILESIA, Province of (Prussia). Or, an eagle displayed sable, crowned and armed of the field, on its breast and wings a crescent and crosslet conjoined argent. Crest — On an oval medallion or, the edge ornamented with peacock feathers proper, the arms of Silesia as above. Stipporters — (Dexter) a savage holding a banner of Prussia ; (sinister) a man in complete armour, on his head a plume of feathers argent and or, holding a banner of the arms of Silesia as above.
