ST. ANDREWS, City of (Fifeshire.) Parted per pale azure and argent, in the dexter on a mount in base the figure of St Andrew proper, bearing his cross in front of him argent, in the sinister growing out of a mount in base an oak-tree proper fructed or, in front of the trunk a boar passant sable, langued gules armed or. Above the shield is placed a mural crown, and on an escrol below the shield this motto, " Dum spiro spero."

[Matriculated in Lyon Register, 29th May 191 2.]
