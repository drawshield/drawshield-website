TASMANIA, See of (Van Diemen's Land and Norfolk Island). Azure, a crosier in bend dexter, surmounting a key in bend sinister or between four stars of eight points argent, the stars as representing the principal constellation of the southern hemisphere, called the Crux Australis. [Granted College of Arms. Gts., xlvi. 150.]
