TAYLORS, Corporation of (Dublin). (Incorporated May 20, 141 7.) Argent, a tent between two maunches gules, on a chief azure, a lamb passant of the first between two "bizants." Crest — On a wreath of the colours, St John the Baptist's head proper in a charger or. Supporters — Two camels proper "bizanted." Motto — " Nudus et operuistus me."

[Granted by Sir Richard Carney, Ulster, July 16, 1684. Original grant is now exhibited in Ulster's Office.]
