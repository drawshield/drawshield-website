THORNABY-ON-TEES (Yorkshire). Barry of twelve gules and argent, on a pale ermine, a lion rampant azure, a chief engrailed of the second, thereon three torteaux. And for the Crest — On a wreath of the colours, in front of two anchors in saltire or, the stern of a ship with a rudder proper. Motto — " Always advancing."

[Granted, College of Arms. 23rd January 1S93.]
