TONGA. No warrant has been issued assigning arms, but the Admiralty publish as "The Royal Standard" a flag quarterly, i. or, three five-pointed stars argent, 2. gules, a crown argent, 3. azure, a dove volant holding in its beak an olive branch argent, 4. or, three clubs heads downwards, two in saltire surmounted by one in pale argent, in the centre of the quarters a six-pointed star argent, charged with a cross couped gules.
