TRINITY HOUSE GUILD OF FRATERNITY.(Incorporated by Henry VIII., 20th May 1515.) Argent, a cross gules between four ships of three masts, each under full sail all proper, on each sail, pennant, and ensign a cross gules, and each quarter representing a sea piece. Crest — On the wreath of the colours, a demi-lion rampant guardant and regally crowned or, holding in the dexter paw a sword erect argent, hiked and pomelled of the first. Motto — "Trinitas in unitate."

[Recorded in the College of Arms.]
