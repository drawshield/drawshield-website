TRURO, See of. Argent on a saltire gules a key, wards upwards [and inwards], surmounted by a sword point downwards, saltierwise or, in base a fleur-de-lis sable, a bordure of the last charged with fifteen bezants. [Granted, College of Arms, 1877.]
