TUNBRIDGE WELLS (Kent). Gules, guttee d'eau, on a pile or, between two fountains in base, a lion rampant gules. Crest — A well proper, issuant therefrom a demi-lion gules, holding between the paws a fountain. Motto —

" Do well, doubt not."

[Granted, College of Arms, 19th July 1889.]
