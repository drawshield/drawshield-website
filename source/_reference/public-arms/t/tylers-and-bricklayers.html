TYLERS AND BRICKLAYERS, The Worshipful Company of, London. (Incorporated 3rd August 1568.) Azure, a chevron or, in chief a fleur-de-hs, argent, between two brick-axes palewise of the second, in base a brush of the last. Mantled — Gules and argent. Crest — On a wreath of the colours, a dexter arm embowed vested per pale or and azure, cuffed argent, holding in the hand proper a brick-axe or. Motto — " In God is all our trust." [Granted by Dethick Garter, 1569.]
