UNIVERSITY, QUEEN'S (Ireland). Argent, a saltire gules, charged with a royal crown of England, between an open ancient book in chief and the Irish harp in base, all proper.

[Granted by Sir W. Betham, Ulster King of Arms, 15th September 1S51.]
