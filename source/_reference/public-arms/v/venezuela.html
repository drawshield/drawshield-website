VENEZUELA, Republic of. Per fesse and the chief per pale dexter gules, a garb or, sinister or, two swords in saltire in front of two flags in saltire all proper, surmounted by a cap of liberty gules, the base azure, on a mount in base vert, a horse courant to the sinister regardant argent.
