VETERINARY SURGEONS, Royal College of. Argent, a cross engrailed gules, between a horse's head erased in the first quarter, an arrow in bend entwined by and piercing a serpent in the second, a horse-shoe in the third, all proper, and a bull's head erased sable in the fourth. Crest — On a wreath of the colours, a centaur proper, holding a shield argent, charged with an aloe also proper Motto—'' Vis unita fortior."

[College of Arms. Gts. xlvii. i66.]
