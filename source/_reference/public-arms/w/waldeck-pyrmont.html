WALDECK-PYRMONT, Principality of. Per pale dexter or, a mullet of eight points sable impaling sinister argent, a cross moline gules. Supporters — Two lions regardant or. Motto — " Palma sub pondere crescit." The full quarterings are as shown in the illustration.
