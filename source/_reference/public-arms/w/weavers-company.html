WEAVERS' COMPANY (Exeter). Per saltire azure and gules, in fesse two shuttles filled palewi.se or, in chief a teazel, in base a pair of sheers lying fesseways argent, on a chief ermine a slea between two burling irons of the third. [Recorded in the College of Arms.]
