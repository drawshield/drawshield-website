WEST HARTLEPOOL (Durham). Has no armorial bearings. Those in use are azure, on a fesse argent, between in chief a bird and in base on waves of the sea a ship under sail, a hart courant between two anchors, all proper. Crest — A demi-hart resting his dexter foot on an anchor reversed. Motto — " E mare ex industria."
