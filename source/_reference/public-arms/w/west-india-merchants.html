WEST INDIA MERCHANTS, Society of. Azure, three ships, hulks, masts, and rigging or, the sails all furled, the pennants and ensigns argent, each charged with a cross gules, on a chief of the second a pale quarterly, viz., ist and 4th, azure, three fleurs-de-lis or ; 2nd and 3rd, gules, three lions passant guardant in pale or, all between two roses of the fourth, seeded of the second, barbed vert.

[Of no authority. Compare with the arms of the East India Company.]
