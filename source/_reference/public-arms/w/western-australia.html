WESTERN AUSTRALIA, State of (Commonwealth of Australia). No warrant assigning arms has as yet been issued to the State of Western Australia, but the following arms are in general use, " Or, a swan naiant sable, the wings elevated and endorsed."

These arms are used upon the Union flag by the Governor but are unauthorised, but refer to Australia.
