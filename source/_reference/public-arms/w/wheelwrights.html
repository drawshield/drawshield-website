WHEELWRIGHTS, The Worshipful Company of, London. (Incorporated 3rd February 1670.) Gules, a chevron between three wheels or, on a chief argent, an axe lying fessewise proper. Crest — On a wreath of the colours, a dexter arm embowed vested gules, cufiTed argent, holding in the hand proper a mallet or. Supporters — Two horses argent. Motto — "God grant unity."
