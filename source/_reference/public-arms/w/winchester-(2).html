WINCHESTER (Hants). Gules, five castles in saltire argent, the centre castle supported by two lions passant guardant or.

Recorded in the College of Arms. In Ulster's Office is a MS. book of the armorial bearings of towns, and the arms, " Sable, three lilies argent, leaved vert.," are given for Winchester. Refer to Winchester College.
