WINCHESTER, See of. Gules, two keys indorsed in bend, the uppermost or, the other argent, a sword interposed between them in bend sinister of the third, pomel and hilt gold.

[Recorded in the College of Arms.]

The Bishop of Winchester, as Prelate of the Order of the Garter, places a Garter round his shield.

These arms first appear on the seal of Bishop William, of Wayneflete,
