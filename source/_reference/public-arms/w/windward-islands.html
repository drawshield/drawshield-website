WINDWARD ISLANDS. No warrant assigning arms has been issued either to the Windward Islands as a whole or to any of the constituent islands, except the island of St Vincent, to which refer. The device published by the Admiralty for use on the Union flag by the Governor contains a crowned escutcheon quarterly gules, or, vert and azure, with the Motto " I pede fausto."
