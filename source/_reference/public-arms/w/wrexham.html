WREXHAM (Denbighshire). Ermine, two crosiers in saltire or, on a chief dancett^ per pale gules and or, two lions passant guardant counterchanged. Ctest — Upon a wreath of the colours, upon a mount vert, a dragon gules, resting the dexter claw upon a shield or, charged with the character of Mars sable. Motto — " Fear God, honour the King."

[Granted by Sir Charles George Young, Knt., Garter Principal King of Arms; J. Piilman, Clarenceux King of Arms; Robert Laurie, Norroy King of Arms, 6th November 1857.]
