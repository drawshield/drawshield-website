WRITERS TO THE SIGNET (Society of Writers to His Majesty's Signet). Azure, a saltire argent, in the flanks two thistles or, and in chief and in base the Royal Signet of the Second with this inscription on the edge, " Signetum Regium."

[Matriculated in Lyon Register, 15th August 1789.]
