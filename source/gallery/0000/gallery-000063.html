Notes

These are the arms of Louis Charles d'Albert, the Duke Luynes, father of Louis-Joseph d'Albert Luynes, the lover of Julie d'Aubigny, known as La Maupin. It was one of the first arms that I had wanted emblazoned. Now that there is a French shield shape that doesn't crop the lower left mullet, Drawshield is handling it perfectly, making it useful and attractive.
