Notes

I combined the shields of the surnames of my parents, Cesar (my father) and Buffa (my mother). The elements from the Cesar shield are: - the eagle argent in middle chief. - the three orange crescents in a gules bar at the middle (in the original shield, they are seashells, but I used crescents because of the similarity) The elements from the Buffa shield are: - the background golden color - the gules lion at the bottom
