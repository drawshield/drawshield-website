    // This coat of armour is made by part of using our
 centuries old family coat of armour and part by adding
 new additions for the Family Moelker van wakeren de
 Bye.
//
// defining the shield
Quarterly // "Quarterly" without any additions defines
 4 quadrants in the coat
1st gules a lion rampant or // First, upper left Quadrant
 Gold lion on Red

2nd or 8 bee sable 2,3,3 // Second, upper right quadrant,
 Black bees on Gold;

3rd // Third, lower left quadrant, Background white/gold
 with a projection of a azure Star of David
gyronny of 16 white and or a star of david azure,
 higher;

4th // Fourth lower right quadrant
quarterly //this quadrant is divided in 4 more sub-quadrants
	i celestial-azure a shofar bronze bendwise dexter
 larger to the dexter; //first sub-quadrant
	ii buff  a corn sheaf or much much smaller, much
 to the dexter;
	iii buff a tower argent masoned sable, much much
 smaller, much much higher to the dexter;
	iv celestial-azure a harp or much much smaller, much
 much higher, much much higher, much to the dexter;

 the coat.

 bordure grey, smaller, lower

 much higher, much higher to the dexter
//..charged with a star of david copper smaller to
 the sinister;
