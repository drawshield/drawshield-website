Notes

Di rosso, alla croce vuota a chiave pomata d’argento. That cross is not in the database. I'm not sure how to translate, my best guess is (word by word): "a cross empty, a key [pomata] argento". "Pomata" (feminin) and "pomato" (masculine) are meant to add small circles to the edges of the cross. https://www.heraldry-wiki.com/heraldrywiki/index.php?title=Pisa
