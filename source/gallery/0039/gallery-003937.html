Blazon

    quarterly  azure and gules  in first quarter 3 fleur
 de lys or  in second quarter 3 lion passant guardant
 in pale or armed and langued azure  in third quarter
 3 lion passant guardant in pale  or armed and langued
 azure  in fourth quarter 3 fleur de lys or over the
 quarters a bend sinister compony argent and  azure
 a bordure counter ermine, an escutcheon sa a chevron
 or charged with five tudor roses proper between three
 leopard heads argent,  supporters a lion rampant or
  a chough proper compartment green, crest Eagle Rising
 sable beaked or armed gules langued gules legged or
 higher smaller, a peer's helmet argent, a torse argent
 and gules, mantling azure and argent, motto "Adsurgo
 Super Omnia"
