Notes

Officially adopted in August 2020, the flag was designed by 9-year old Calum Alasdair Munro. The five oars (cricket bats) on the ship represent the five areas on Skye which are Trotternish, Waternish, Duirinish, Minginish and Sleat. https://www.bbc.com/news/uk-scotland-highlands-islands-53887936
