Blazon

    quarterly I Per Fess argent and gules a lion rampant smaller
 within a bordure counter changed impaled with Sable
 three chevronels between three mullets Argent

 counterchanged impaled with sable, a anchor fouled
 argent, two Moon Increscent respectant argent in fess
 very much smaller much higher, a chief rayonny argent
III Gules a pale or impaled with sable a lion argent
 crowned or langued gu
IV per pale dancetty sable and or  3 chevronels sable
 in dexter flank a eagle or a label entire semy de
 lys white and sanguine
over quarters a escutcheon argent, a Phoenix sable
 beaked and inflamed or langued gules
