--- pageTitle: Coat of Arms of Canada layout: gallery-entry entryNum: 007230 --- 
    Tierced in fess, the first Gules three lions passant
 guardant in pale Or, impaling Or a lion rampant within
 a double tressure flory-counter-flory Gules, the second
 Azure a harp Or stringed Argent, impaling Azure three
 fleurs-de-lis Or, third Argent three maple leaves
 in fess gules, royal helmet Or, crest a royal crown
 Or, mantling gules and argent, supporters a lion Or
 and a unicorn Argent armed, crined and unguled Or,
 compartment vert, motto "A mari usque ad mare".
