Notes

This is one of three documented shields for spanish surename Ardila, probably toponimic from the river of the same name, at the south of the region of Extremadura. During the colonial period a branch was stablished in Colombia and became there over the years much more prevalent than it was in Spain. The other two documented blazons being: (Ardila 2) argent two bundles of grapes gules (Ardila 3) purple a lion rampant or
