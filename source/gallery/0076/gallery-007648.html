--- pageTitle: Coat of Arms of Cuba layout: gallery-entry entryNum: 007648 --- 
    Per pale, the first bendy sinister of five argent and
 azure, the second bleu celeste, a trimount tenne,
 a palm tree proper; crest a phrygian cap gules charged
 with a mullet of five points argent, supporters two
 palm leaves vert.
