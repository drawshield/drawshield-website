Notes

A coat of arms made for a TTRPG campaign. The Viera are rabbit people based on the Scottish highlanders. They fight like the Gallowglass and usually serve as elite troops and foot guards for elvish generals of the Tiawarin Empire. Their motto represents their warrior mindset. They are rabbits, but they are not a prey. They don't fear you, you should fear them.
