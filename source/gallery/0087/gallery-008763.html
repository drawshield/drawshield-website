Notes

The cadet family of primitive Lancasters (they are from Broughton, the part of county of Salisbury, now in the county of Hampshire) in the 5th century a.D. Founded in the year 497 AD. in a new village that had a tree petrifed this brach has as pratiarch and founder: Lucius "Magnus Gladius" Britannicus Gallius Lancaster, the "Golded Eagle" or "The Indomitable". Great warrior and proto-knight who predated King Arthur by 40 years.
