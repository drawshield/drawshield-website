Notes

Combination of my family's crest and the town where we lived for many generations. I added some historical elements, the plough representing my numerous farmer ancestors, the hammer for the carpenters and manual laborers. The musket to represent my great-great-great-great-great-grandfather and his brother who both were captains during the 1838 french canadian patriots rebellion.
