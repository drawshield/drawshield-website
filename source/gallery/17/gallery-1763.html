    Quartered of 6
I. Quarterly 1st and 4th Or, a leopard gules armed
 and langued azure 2nd and 3rd Gules, a leopard or
 armed and langued azure (Wales)
II. Or, a lion gules armed and langued azure shown
 shorter and much smaller within a double tressure
 flory-counter-flory gules (Scotland)
III. Sable, fifteen bezants (Cornwall)
IV. Ermine (Brittany)
V. Azure, a harp or (Ireland)
VI. Gules, a triskelion armoured argent shown very
 much raised (Isle of Man)
