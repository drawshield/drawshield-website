    Quarterly
1st Gules, a castle triple towered or
2nd Argent, a lion purpure armed and langued gules
3rd Or, four endorses gules
4th Gules, a chain of Navarre or gemmed vert shown
 very much raised
Over the quarters enty in point argent charged with
 a pomegranate or fructed gules leaved vert shown lowered
 and larger; a Spanish shaped escutcheon Azure, three
 fleurs-de-lys fancy or, a bordure gules; shown larger

Achievement crest a royal crown shown very much larger,
 raised, much narrower; supporters two pillars argent
 shown taller, very much narrower, very much raised;
 motto scroll "PLVS VLTRA" shown very much raised
