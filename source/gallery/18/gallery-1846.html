    Grand-quarterly
I. and IV. Quarterly 1st and 4th Gules, a chinese
 dragon or shown larger; 2nd and 3rd Azure, two falchions
 in saltire argent hilted and pommeled or shown very
 much larger; over the quarters an escutcheon Or, 
 three fleurs-de-lis or on a bend azure, a bordure
 sable
II. Quarterly 1st and 4th Vert, two lions rampant
 guardant combattant or armed and langued gules crowned
 or shown raised and shorter, a fleur-de-lis or in
 nombril; 2nd and 3rd Sable, two battle axes addorsed
 or, a label of 5 entire argent
III. Azure, a chevron or, three bear heads erased
 or, a bordure compony purpure and argent; impaled
 with Ermine, an eagle displayed gules, armed, beaked,
 langued, and legged or crowned or, a bordure embattled
 gules
Over the quarters shown larger an escutcheon Purpure,
 a bendlet sinister celestial azure, a unicorn rampant
 argent shown slightly smaller, a bordure compony sable
 and or
