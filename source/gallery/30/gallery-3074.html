Notes

My official homemade coat of arms. The 1st quarter represents my hometown, Hue. The 2nd and 3rd quarter has bordure sable, it is my mother's region of origin - The North. Not just that, the dog refers to my age according to Chinese zodiac system. The 4th quarter represents my religion - Christianity, the fleur-de-lys argent is the attribute of my 2 patron saints, saint Joseph and saint Casimir.
