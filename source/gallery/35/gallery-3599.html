Notes

VBCW is a table top game that takes place in the inter war period. In this scenario the Uk collapses into a civil war with several factions fighting for power over the UK. One of these is the neo-Jacobites based in northern Scotland. Ruprecht Wittelsbach is the crown prince of Bavaria at the time who also happens to be related to the Stuarts. Ruprecht could flee nazi-controlled Germany (he was apparently staunchly anti-nazi) and try to claim the throne of the UK in the name of the Stuart dynesty.
