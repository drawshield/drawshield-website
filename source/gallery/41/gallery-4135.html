Notes

A hypothetical coat of arms if Tankred of Kovir and Poviss married Cirilla Fionna of Cintra. First and fourth quarter: Arms of Kovir and Poviss impaled with the arms of Cintra. Second: quarerly Arms of Aedrin, quarterly arms of Cidaris and its vassal Bremervoord, arms of the Hengefores League, arms of Kaedwen Third: quarterly, arms of Rivia and Lyria, quarterly arms of Temeria and its vassals Brugge, Mahakam, Sodden, and in the escutcheon the arms of Ellander and Maribor, the arms of Redania, the arms of Verden.
