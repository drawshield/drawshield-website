--- pageTitle: Personal arms Joshua VALLAUX. layout: gallery-entry entryNum: 4316 --- 
    Per cross vert and argent, in chief two rabbits courant
 addorsed tenne, in base a rabbit courant slightly
 higher tenne, over all in fess an open book argent
 fimbriated sable clasped or higher. Mantling vert
 and pean, a helmet argent, torse vert and argent,
 crest a passion nail gules smaller. Motto "salvum
 fac temet ipsum".
