Notes

this is obviously not a real flag of Ancient Greece because it was split into different city-states which all had their own banner. Also that sort of variant edge type does not exist in heraldry, I just saw it in the visual catalogue and I got inspired to make a Greek-like flag, or one that I would have imagined being used if there was such a thing as the Unified City-States of Ancient Greece.
