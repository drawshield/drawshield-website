    Quarterly:
	1st, Gules, a patriarchal cross Argent, a mount of
 three peaks Azure.
    2nd, barry of 7 Azure and or, impaled with Argent,
 a bear rampant Gules, larger.
    3rd, Azure, an eagle chequy Argent and Gules,
 crowned Or, armed, legged and langued Or, larger.
    4th, Or, an eagle sable crowned Or, armed and
 langued Gules, larger.
Over quarters an escutcheon Gules much larger higher,
 a lion rampant Argent crowned Or, armed and langued
 Or, larger higher.
Supporters two lions rampant Or, langued Gules. Motto
 "PRAVDA VÍTĚZÍ" Azure much higher higher
