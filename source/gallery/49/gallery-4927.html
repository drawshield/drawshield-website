--- pageTitle: Coat of Arms of Slovenia layout: gallery-entry entryNum: 4927 --- 
    Azure, a mountain argent surmounted by two bars wavy
 azure, in chief three mullets of six points Or two
 and one, a bordure gules.

With Tags: Slovenia

Notes

Since the government doesn't have a blazon for it, I decided to make it based on the elements seen. Note that the bordure has a top, which doesn't appear on the official render.
