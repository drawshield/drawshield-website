    or, on a chief rayonny per pale azure and gules a winged
 cap much larger between two towers smaller or a whale
 spouting azure langued gules spouting gules smaller
  much higher
motto "FINEM RESPICE" much higher

Notes

Winged cap should actually be stated as a winged "conical helmet," but such is nit currently available. Whale is langued gules. However, the whale depicted has no tongue. Tassels of the manteling, though not depicted, are specifically "fox tails azure bezanty."
