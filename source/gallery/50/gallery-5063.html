    per pale or and azure, on a chief embattled, argent
 checky sable,
a bulls head gules very much larger and lower
a vine vert stick sable very much to the dexter, much
 smaller, slightly higher
3 barrels or hooped argent in pale fesswise very much
 to the sinister, slightly smaller
achievement mantling buff and vert esquires helmet
 steel higher motto "By earth, tree, and bone."
