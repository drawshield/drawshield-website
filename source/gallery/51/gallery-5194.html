Notes

It's my imaginary Habsburg Netherlands coat of arms. Sable a lion or, represents Brabant region Fleury azure and or with label represents County of Artois Or a lion sable with riband gules represents County of Namur Or a lion sable represents Flandres region Or a lion gules represents County of Hainaut Escutcheon in the center of the entire coat of arms is Habsburg Dynasty crest There are also supporters: A lion or- symbol of southern Netherlands and an griffin sable- symbol of Austria-Hungary Hope you like this coat of arms ;), making it took a lot of work.
