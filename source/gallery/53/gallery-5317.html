Notes

The Knot has four points symbolizing the four provinces of Ireland, the Colors are the national Colors of the Kingdom of Ireland and the Elk is the creature that appears on many Irish Coat of Arms as Supporters, the Motto "Comhairle An Ceart" means "Advice of the Right" which was me trying to google how to say "Advise the Right" in Irish.
