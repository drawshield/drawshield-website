    Per fess barry bendy sinister gules and or and or,
 in nombril a wheel sable, in fess point a cap of mercury
 sable shown much lower

With Tags: German Gemeinde, or, barry bendy sinister, wheel, cap of mercury

Notes

This one I had to get a little silly with. The real coat shows a spinning, winged wheel with a gold rim and hub, but as sthe wings are a reference to mercury, I just gave it a little hat rather than trying to recreate it.
