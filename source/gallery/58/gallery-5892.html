    Tierced in pairle bendy counter ermine and paly ermine
 and erminois, or, and celestial azure,
A cross masoned gules and argent,
A butterfly tenne in sinister base,
A seahorse vert in dexter base,
A rose murrey in dexter chief,
A sinister gyron azure,
A ram bisque.
