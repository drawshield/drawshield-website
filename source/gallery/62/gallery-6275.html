    Quarterly of Sixteen, I, VI, XI, XVI gules a fess argent
 impaling barry of eight gules and argent; II, V, XII,
 XV vert a panther rampant argent; IV, VII, X, XIII
 argent an eagle displated azure, armed, beaked, and
 langued gules, charged with a crescent chequy gules
 and or impaling chequy gules and argent; III and IX
 azure a goat statant or unguled and armed gules impaling
 gules a spear argent; XIV and VIII barry of seven
 azure and or impaling argent a bear rampant gules;
 mantling sable and or; helmet murrey; supporters two
 griffins gules; compartment shelf or; motto "Mein
 Gott und Mein Land"; crest an eagle sable crowned
 tenne
