--- pageTitle: Coat of Arms of Royal Reandor layout: gallery-entry entryNum: 6530 --- 
    Per fess sanguine and murrey per saltire counterchanged
 on a chief embattled sable five pine cones gules inverted,
two hawks grady azure and or respecting each other,
a chevron sable abased voided,
in abyss a tower or smaller.
