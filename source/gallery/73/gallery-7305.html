Blazon

    Sable a lion or tongued and armed gules shown larger
 (which is for Belgium), an escutcheon barry of ten
 sable and or a crancelin vert bendwise sinister shown
 smaller to the dexter and lower (which is for Wettin).
Achievement a royal helmet or, crest a royal crown
 or shown higher, mantling of ribbons sable and or.
