    vert
chief argent
in chief three mullets gules
in fess point an apple gules leaved or smaller higher
in nombril a killer whale sable larger larger larger
 lower

With Tags: washington

Notes

An imaginary shield I made for my home state which doesn't have one. The three mullets refer to George Washington's personal coat of arms as the state is named after him. The main color of the field an the two charges are prominent symbols of Washington State.
