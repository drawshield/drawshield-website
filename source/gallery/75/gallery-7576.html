    Argent, a chevron engrailed sable charged with an estoile
 of six or very much larger between three dog's heads
 of the second langued gules slightly smaller; torse
 argent and sable, mantling argent and sable, crest
 a dog's head sable bezanty langued gules; motto "JACTA
 EST ALEA"
