--- pageTitle: An alternate coat of arms for Canada layout: gallery-entry entryNum: 7912 --- 
    quarterly first azure a turtle vert; second azure three
 fleur-de-lys or; third gules three leopards or armed
 and langued azure impaled with Or a lion rampant within
 a double tressure flory-counter-flory Gules;; fourth
 azure a harp or impaled with or a wyvern azure armed
 and langued gules;; over all parts a French escutcheon
 argent three maple leaves gules

Notes

Turtle----symbolizes the First Nations, who called this land Turtle Island Fleur-de-lys----symbolizes French heritage Leopards and lion rampant----England and Scotland Harp and Wyvern----Ireland and Chinese(Sorry, I cannot find a Chinese dragon, so I use a wyvern as an alternative here) Maple Leaves----Canada
