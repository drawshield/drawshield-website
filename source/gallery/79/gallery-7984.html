    per fess engrailed argent and azure
a caravel sable shown much raised and smaller

With Tags: dnd greyhawk oerth oreik flanaess keoland

Notes

Based on Anna B. Meyer heraldry of Greyhawk, but adhering to heraldry rules. Opted for engrailed rather than invected because, well, the azure bottom is supposed to be the sea. Didn't have an idea about how to represent the... ummm... fishing net?
