    Per pale gules and azure,
A griffin or slightly smaller slightly lower,
A star argent very much smaller much raised raised
 very much to the dexter much to the dexter,
Mantling azure and gules,
Achievment a peers helmet argent,
Motto "EXERCITO - 1648 - BRASILEIRO"
