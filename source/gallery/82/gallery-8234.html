    or
a cross flory sable
a bend gules
on a bend engrailed or three fireballs proper bendwise
a chief wavy per fess argent and azure
a ship proper in dexter chief higher
a palm tree proper in middle chief higher higher a
 little higher
a tower proper in sinister chief higher
