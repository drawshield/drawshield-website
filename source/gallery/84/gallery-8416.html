Notes

Original blazon: Ar. a saltire sa. in base a holly leaf vert. Crest - A stag ppr. attired of ten tynes ar. lodged before a holly bush also ppr. Motto - Reviresco Changed holly bush to a "Elder Sprig Fructed" due to lack of matching charges. Also stag lodged is lacking some of its tynes and unable to change their color. Source: The General Armory of England, Scotland, Ireland, and Wales; Comprising a Registry of Armorial Bearings from the Earliest to the Present Time
