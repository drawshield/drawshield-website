    Per Saltire Azure and Vert a saltire argent a hand
 gules smaller smaller smaller

Notes

The red hand symbols Aodh O'Neill, ruler of Tyrone and Leader of Nine Years' War. Saltire symbols the Scottish people who settled in Ulster afterwards. Green symbols Catholics, Blue symbols Protestants.
