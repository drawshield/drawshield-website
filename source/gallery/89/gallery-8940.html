Suggested by an anonymous user, who adds:

Sorry for the ugly blazon! Fimbriating two ordinaries together requires one to be redrawn on top, but this thins the fimbriation on that one, requiring the second to be redrawn to even the thickness overall. My simplified blazon to be used elsewhere would be: Azure a tierce and engrailed fess both gules and fimbriated sable, in fess three annulets sable, in sinister chief two crescents argent, and in sinister base two more inverted.
