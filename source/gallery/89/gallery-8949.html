    sa a fret arg on a canton arg a square shaped escutcheon
 per fess enhanced chequy or and gu and vair much larger
 lower to the sinister slightly to the sinister
(Harington differenced with Chichester)

Notes

Anne (1605 - 1627/8), daughter of Sir Robert Chichester of Raleigh (1578-1627), by his first wife, Frances Harington (1587 - 1622) who was coheir of her brother John Harington, 2nd Baron Harington of Exton, county Rutland (1592-1614). (Heir to her mother but not to her father).
