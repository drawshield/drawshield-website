    Gules, 
  a bend sinister sable 
    fimbriated argent, 
  in dexter chief an eagle of the second
    lower much larger
-- Flag of Ipsylvania

Notes

In junior high school, friends of mine and I each made up a cryptic nation. Mine was the Romani nation of Ipsylvania, located between Albania and Greece. This is the flag of Ipsylvania, based on the arms of the Royal House Gregori. (https://drawshield.net/gallery/98/gallery-9876.html)
