    Quarterly
1st quarterly i and iv or a lion passant gardant gules,
 ii and iii gules a lion passant gardant or;;
2nd azure three bundles of corn or

4th quarterly i and iv Or, a fess chequy argent and
 azure, ii and iii argent a galley sable;;
