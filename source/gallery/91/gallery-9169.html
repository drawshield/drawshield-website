    Quarterly first sable on a cross argent five mullets
 of the first.
Second Gules on a cross or, five roses proper, on
 a chief azure three harps or stringed argent larger
Third per bend vert and sable, a castle triple towered
 argent in fess larger larger larger lower.
Fourth azure, an eagle displayed higher erminois impaled
 with or a book sable.
