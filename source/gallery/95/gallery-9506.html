    Sable 
        upon a roundel or an ouroboros vert 
        a flames gules shown much smaller

Notes

Shield of the New Badoual bard college sisterhood, whose members focuses on the study of draconic "humanities". Not a house per se, but when they take their vows to enter the order they must disown their former family to focus completely on academic research. To fill this void the sorority takes on a familial role with even stronger bonds, members proudly flying banners at events citywide.
