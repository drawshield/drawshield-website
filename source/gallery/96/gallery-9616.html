    quarterly
1st, azure, a pall invected argent between three fleur de lis or
2nd, chequy or and gules, a ship sable
3rd, sable, a pale or cotticed, in pale three fish gules smaller
4th, argent, a bend dexter wavy azure, in bend four fishhooks sable,

Notes

*The invection of the pall in the first quarter is meant to look like rope*
