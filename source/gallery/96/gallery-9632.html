  seme de lys or 
    a cross-formy gules
    fimbriated argent

    -- Alice Margaret Philipa Saint-Clair
    -- "Countess Alice Faircliffe"
    -- Heir of Roslin Poindexter, the Duchess of Faircliffe and Aumale

Suggested by an anonymous user, who adds:

Arms from Earl Wajenberg's sundered nation of Grand Normandy, at least according to her mother, Roslin Poindexter, the Duchess of Faircliffe and Aumale. Her father would prefer the duchies of Aumale and Faircliffe merge, to be inherited by his son.
