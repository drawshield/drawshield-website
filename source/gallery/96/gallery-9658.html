    [Arms of Roger de Mortimer de Chirk, c.1256 - 3 August 1326,  as blazoned in the Roll of Caerlaverock

Epuis Rogiers de Mortemer,             
Ki, deca mer e dela mer,               
A porté quel part ke ait alé           
L'escu barré au chief palé             
E les cornieres gyronnées,             
De or e de asur enluminées,            
O le escuchon vuidie de ermine.        
     
i.e. barry of six or and azure, on a chief of the first two pallets between two base esquierres of the second, overall an inescutcheon ermine

The best way to achieve this in Drawshield appears to be:]

barry of 6 or and azure 
an escutcheon arg smaller very much higher
a chief paly of 9 or and azure a canton and canton sinister per bend or and azure
an escutcheon smaller much higher arg 6 ermine spots in fess point sa higher
