This example demonstrates that DrawShield can draw different edge types (about 20 variations altogether). These can be applied to most of the larger ordinaries and divisions. The bars argent invected against an azure field look a bit like vapour trails to me and I offer this suggestions to any pilots that may wish to register their own arms!
